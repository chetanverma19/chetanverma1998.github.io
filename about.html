Aman Exports was established by Deepak Verma in the year 1994 at New Delhi. The firm started as a small manufacturing unit producing Metal Shelving. Patronized by Mr. T.J. Baylis & Mrs. C.J. Baylis owners of RB UK the firm grew leaps and bounds. A new factory was built at Noida during 1996 and production commenced in 1997. The new factory with modern machinery increased production many fold. New product ranges were introduced. Having completed 20 years we are taking another leap into the future by going for automation now, Aman Export's team comprises of experienced & dedicated Professionals. This rich experience along with the desire to excel, has given Aman Exports, an edge over competetion.
